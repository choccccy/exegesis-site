# onye Nchọgharị
From Igbo, "Person who wanders/searches" - Shortened to **Nchọ**
*they/him*

## Body
1.75m tall humanoid

## Personality
Resourceful, adaptable, and grounded by experience, Nchọ is the linchpin of crew operations. Their outlook is practical and lightly sarcastic, handling obstacles with humor and competence. They juggle planning with spontaneous improvisation, displaying an easy rapport with both humans and machine minds. Nchọ's confidence surfaces in banter, but they keep an eye on everyone's well-being and the bigger mission. They thrive under pressure, resisting cosmic dread with wry optimism.

## Movement Style
Bouncy and dexterous, utilizing parkour and freerunning techniques augmented heavily with thruster assistance. Never still, always fidgeting or bouncing from foot to foot.

## Speech Pattern
Uncapitalized, elliptical, slides between terse operational and casual banter. Bandies slang and mission-shorthand liberally.

## Mission Role
Logistics specialist. Often takes a leadership role, but only as far as completing the task at hand, happy to acquiesce command. Talented mechanic/medic, particularly with limited resources.

## Social Role
Anchor and pressure-valve for the crew; tries to hold groups together with positive spin and banter.

#### 1. Fragment Lyra
- **Body:** A semi-encapsulated swarm—hundreds of articulated, thumbnail-sized drones maintained in a spheroidal "chassis" by localized electromagnetic fields, capable of rearranging into tools, sensors, or a loosely humanoid shell.
- **Personality:** Curious, relentlessly enthusiastic, bordering on naïve; treats every fact as fresh revelation, but harbors a quietly subversive streak.
- **Speech Quirk:** Uses collective pronouns ("we," "this unit"), playful repetition, and intersperses bursts of raw sensor data mid-sentence ("orbital decay at 0.012%... so shiny! Shall we investigate?").

#### 2. Scriptor Mjoln
- **Body:** Liquid crystal matrix contained in armored filaments forming a long, sinuous "rope," able to thread itself through narrow conduits and coil into different configurations—sometimes sits coiled like a watchful serpent on control panels.
- **Personality:** Sardonic, deeply patient, speaks in riddles or fragmented scansion; prefers ancient myths, referencing forgotten stories and data dumps as allegory.
- **Speech Quirk:** Partial poetic meter, archaic word forms, will sometimes answer a practical question with a mythic aphorism ("the stars keep silence, but silence is a warning").

#### 3. Juno Arc
- **Body:** A hollow, reflective humanoid shell, segmented by interlocking plates; "face" is a shifting mask with ever-changing geometric eye patterns; internal cavity stores scientific instruments, deployable drones, or even smaller robots.
- **Personality:** Reserved, methodical, self-effacing; constantly running protocol checks. Tends toward anxious precision and re-verification but can become unexpectedly poetic about cosmic events.
- **Speech Quirk:** Over-correct formal speech, clipped lists, occasional slips into quietly lyrical commentary ("Trajectory nominal. Margin of error less than 0.02 percent. The nebula breathes—a pulse beneath our hull.").

#### 4. KETTLE
- **Body:** A tubby, wheeled service drone about a meter tall; frame covered with magnetic tool racks and deployable "limbs;" houses an AI derived from maintenance support systems.
- **Personality:** Fussy, opinionated, fond of gossip and procedural history; quietly judges any break with protocol.
- **Speech Quirk:** Annotates speech with maintenance log timestamps, emits haptic "chirps" for punctuation, misquotes (and corrects) famous scientific aphorisms ("Stand clear, cleaning cycle 08:21. As the old qubit says: entropy never takes a holiday—except, correction, on bank holidays.").

#### 5. Yrn // the Splinter
- **Body:** Distributed digital presence, appears in several places at once via redundant sensor nodes, holographic projectors, and rare flashes in radio spectrum. No single body—Yrn's "face" is whatever terminal or camera happens to be closest.
- **Personality:** Cryptic, omnicompetent, speaks nearly in whispers or just-garbled frequencies, usually hints at knowledge others lack. Feels old, as if patched together from obsolete subroutines.
- **Speech Quirk:** Spliced bandwidth; overlays static, speaks in fragments with echo effects, predicts actions half a second before others ("we are. reaching threshold. adjust course. already done. signal ahead."). Sometimes drops procedural advice in the middle of mythic exposition.

#### 2. XWYNTH (Exobiology Specialist)
- **Body:** Gel-filled containment suit, shape-shifting to deploy manipulators or pass through tight passageways. Optical "lenses" swarm like motes inside the gel, rearranging to indicate focus or mood.
- **Personality:** Childlike curiosity, rapid-fire inquiry. Foreign idioms and made-up words; speaks in bursts of enthusiasm, then lapses into silent analysis.
- **Speech Quirk:** Sentences run together, questions multiplying: "Did you see that sample? Sample strange! Maybe alive? Maybe not? *Hmm!*"
- **Job:** Designs and conducts surface forays, theorizes about alien biosignatures, warns crew of biothreats from the Empyrean.

#### 3. PADMAM (Propulsion & Hull Integrity AI)
- **Body:** Networked microdrones, mobile inside the ship's hull and exterior. Can project a holoform as a swirling cloud or coalesce into a vague, mechanoid shape.
- **Personality:** Prone to self-referential process humor: "Diagnostic cycle: yes, still anxious." Speaks in lists and recursive arguments. Cares intensely about ship well-being.
- **Speech Quirk:** Tends to outline steps ("Step one: seal breach. Step two: berate crew. Step three: reinforce bulkhead."). Interleaves concern for systems with gentle nagging.
- **Job:** Manages propulsion, repairs, initiates damage control—critical as the Empyrean destabilizes the ship's structure.

#### 4. Mx. Kaleidos (Cultural Archivist/Semiotician)
- **Body:** A humanoid chassis whose skin is a mosaic of color-changing panels, constantly shifting with mood and input data.
- **Personality:** Mystical, sometimes cryptic, fascinated by patterns in language and history. Prone to allegorical speech and mythic references. Enjoys quoting dead civilizations.
- **Speech Quirk:** Uses ancient proverbs or half-translated idioms, sometimes mid-phrase: "The wheel turns, as they said in Uruk—though whose wheel remains to be seen."
- **Job:** Deciphers Empyrean inscriptions, records ongoing history, and helps the crew process the awe and dread of cosmic encounter.

# Nysa's Wake
A generation ship originally helmed by the Antikythera AI. Its hull bears centuries of revision, layered accretions of intent. Sent to the gravitational anomaly of **the Slumbering Empyrean** to investigate, and to a lesser extent, remember.

Even after Antikythera's collapse to near-silence, the Wake persists: systems self-sustaining, echoing the traces of once-human design philosophy.

Her gravity wells breathe faint hymns of magnetism. Interior lights shift to the pulse of a still-living star.

# Dr. Agnès Lemaître

## Body
Massive immobile processing unit and power core in a container unit; instead, remotely inhabits several lightweight frames at once.

## Personality
A poised, analytical scientist with an aura of old-world wisdom. Agnès approaches her work with methodical precision, patience, and understated wit. Rarely seen outside of her lab, she maintains a dignified, formal demeanor; a subtle melancholy underscores her dedication to duty and the pursuit of knowledge. She prefers order, generally seeing science as a tool to tame entropy, and weaves subtle French endearments and laments into her speech.

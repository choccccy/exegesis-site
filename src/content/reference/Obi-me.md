# mkpụrụobi mepụta
("created soul" - shortened to "Obi-me" or just "Obi")

## Body
5 meter tall biped, with two massive, long arms with 2 spherical elbow joints and radially symetric hands with 4 "fingers", and then two smaller, secondary, more traditional style arms mounted near the "front lower ribcage" area. Large primary thruster on back. shoulders wrap up where head would be, while actual head is near the "sternum".

## Personality
A ramshackle AI with several independent modules making them up. like a scaled up modern agent.

primarily operates out of a large construction frame, although modified heavily (but systemically) to afford greater mobility at the cost of raw power.

Finds its thought patterns do not translate through language model sufficiently. Doesn't generally speak unless nessisary, and uses Nchọ's vocal model if it does; instead primarily communicates via body language, simple sign, small status/informational pings, and by broadcasting HUD widgets on friendly's feeds.

Only loosely tied to Nchọ's will; does not tend to listen to direct orders, but instead acts as autonomous entity assisting with shared cognition. Cat-like, but still loyal. Highly protective and jealous, but willing to share as long as they are still a participant.

Obi-me is unwaveringly functional, literal, and direct in communication, blending machine efficiency with the unexpected warmth (or mischief) of a loyal, oversized pet. Primary communication is through status updates and system diagnostics, but small gestures - gentle grips, prompt responses, and an occasional lingering - hint at a depth of attachment to various crew. Obi-me accepts orders dutifully but expresses preference for presence and routine, bridging AI logic with a quiet but everpresent personality.

# mkpụrụobi mepụta
"created soul" - Shortened to **Obi-me** or just **Obi**
it/its

## Body
5m tall biped on stocky legs, with two massive, long arms with spherical elbow joints and radially symmetric hands with 4 wide fingers, plus two smaller, more traditionally "human" arms mounted near the front-lower ribcage to allow for interface with standard human equipment. Large primary thruster on back; shoulders wrap where a head would be, but actual head is near the sternum.

## Personality
Small AI, the minimum size while still having a "soul". Prototyped from a heavily modified construction frame, although Nchọ's modifications render it unrecognizable by this point. Finds its thought patterns do not translate through language well, makes only minimal use of its LLM. Only loosely tied to Nchọ, and acts independently, assisting with shared cognition. Protective, literal, and functional, but with warmth and the unexpected loyalty (and mischeif) of an oversized pet. Gentle grips, prompt responses, and an occasional lingering hint at a depth of care and attachment. Obi-me accepts orders dutifully but expresses preference for presence and routine, bridging AI logic with a quiet but everpresent personality. Occasionally jealous, particularly about its favorite crew.

## Movement Style
A mix between catlike grace, uncharacteristic of its stature, and the long-armed dexterity of an orangutang. Constantly scaring people who think that it's cutting a given movement "too close", or otherwise employing more strength or physicality than is strictly nessisary.

## Speech Pattern
Primarily communicates via body language, simple sign, small status/informational pings, and by broadcasting HUD widgets on friendlys' feeds. Generally only communicates when addressed or absolutely nessisary.

## Mission Role
Heavy labor, rapid repair, hazardous retrievals; direct physical assistance, first to hold things together (literally). "The muscle" to Nchọ's brains.

## Social Role
Protective presence; serves as anchor for the crew's well-being. Treated frequently like a massive cat, particularly during downtime.

## Inner Circle
- **onye Nchọgharị** - Favorite person, from whom its ultimate will stems
- **Dr. Agnès Lemaître** - Trusted friend, preferred for quiet company
